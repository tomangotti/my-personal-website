My story

I am originally from West Virginia which is where I went to college at West Virginia University and graduated with a bachelors of Science in Civil Engineering. I move to Denver just over 6 years ago and started my career in the construction industry. I was hired by Rocky Mountain Prestress in 2016. I worked there for a few years holding multiple positions from Quality Control Inspector to Design Engineer. In 2019 Rocky Mountain Prestress was purchased by Wells Concrete and I continued working for them for another 3 years as a Design Engineer.
